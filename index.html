Your Cozy Escape in Kanatal

Nestled in the serene hills of Kanatal at 8,500 feet, Mountain Retreat Homestay offers a perfect blend of comfort and nature. Wake up to breathtaking views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and experience the warmth of traditional Garhwali hospitality.

Our family-run homestay provides an authentic experience away from the hustle of city life. Enjoy home-cooked meals, cozy rooms with mountain views, and the simple pleasure of doing nothing at all.
